Games

Your Cards

Each card is double-sided. One side is blue, one side is red to track ownership. Each card has 4 numbers that add up to 16.

Tic Tac Toe

Two players, 5 cards each

1

One player is Red, one is Blue. Each picks 5 cards to play with.

2

Take turns placing one card face-up in your colour on a 3×3 grid.

3

First to get three of their colour in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.

Card Battle

Two players, 5 cards each

1

Draw a 3×3 grid. Decide who goes first.

2

On your turn, place one card in any empty cell, face-up in your colour.

3

Compare numbers with any adjacent cards — the number on the edge touching the neighbouring card is what counts. If your number is higher, flip that card to your colour. It's now yours.

4

Flipped cards can be flipped again on future turns. Play continues until all 9 cells are filled.

5

Count the cards in your colour on the board to determine the winner.
